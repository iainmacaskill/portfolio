About

I am a delivery manager and servant leader. I create the conditions for teams to deliver value: collaboration, self-organisation, transparency, and the discipline to measure results rather than guess at them.

I cannot write code, and that is rather the point of this site. Everything below was built by describing outcomes clearly to AI tools, then governing what came back: testing it, documenting it, and deciding what it is not allowed to do. The same question decides whether any AI is worth trusting at work: not "how much can it produce?" but "what is it not allowed to do, and who signs it off?"
